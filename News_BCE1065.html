Pongal Celebrations

posted on 10 jan | vit chennai

The Pongal celebrations at VIT Chennai were conducted grandly and saw a very good turnout from students across all years.

A village-themed setup was created inside the campus which gave a festive and traditional feel. Many students enjoyed the decorations, music, and overall atmosphere.

The event was well organised and received positive feedback from students. Overall, it added joy and colour to the campus during the Pongal season.

Campus Highlights

CAT exams start from 21st January

CAT exams for the winter semester will begin from 21st January. Hall details will be available on VTOP two days before the exam.

Another Day, Another Queue

Students stand in long queues for entry, mess food, and buses, especially during morning and lunch hours. Many feel the system can be organised better.

Wi-Fi Issue During Online Work

Some students faced Wi-Fi issues during online quizzes and submissions. This caused confusion and stress during important activities.

Hostel Mess Menu Confusion

Hostel mess served a different menu this week. While some liked it, many students were confused about the food items. Clear menu display was requested.

Attendance Portal Issue

Attendance portal was slow and not opening properly for some students. This caused worry as exams are approaching.
